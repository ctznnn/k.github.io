    A NEW STYLE OF A CLASSIC ADVENTURE

    A link to the past! Relive your greatest adventures with Guardian Tales! From solving challenging puzzles in epic dungeons to strategic action combat, embark on this legendary journey to save the world of Kanterbury!

    Puzzle Solving Gameplay

    Lift heavy boulders, throw explosive bombs and sling yourself across obstacles to discover hidden pathways to amazing treasures!

    Strategic Action Combat

    Dodge, duck, dip, dive and dodge your way to victory against powerful foes and massive bosses!

    Challenging Dungeons & Bosses

    Explore dark and dangerous dungeons to challenge the behemothic bosses that reside within! Evil pig monsters beware, the hero has arrived!

    Intense PVP and Rankings

    Assemble and synergize your best party of 3 heroes to face others in real-time combat for glory!

    Collect and Enhance Weapons

    It's dangerous to go alone! Collect and choose from over 100 different weapons - each with their own unique abilities!

    Create a Guild with Friends

    Make new friends, show off your heroes and party it up in the Guild House! Oh, and go easy on the Guild Scarecrow...

    Customize Your Castle

    You like pancakes? Build a Pancake House! Love clowns? Build a Circus! Customize your island the way you want for you and your heroes!

    COLLECT AND MASTER YOUR HEROES

    With over 50 unique heroes to collect, master their skills and maximize their stats to overwhelm your enemies! Synergize your party composition to amplify your team's overall damage.
